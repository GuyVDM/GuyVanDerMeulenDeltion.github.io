About me:

Hey there! My name is Guy van der Meulen and i am currently 18 years old, I study GameDevelopment at Deltion College Zwolle. Gamedesign has been on my mind since I was a little kid, and was always inspired by the concept in it's entirety. I played alot of console games, especially the ones from Nintendo like the Zelda & Donkey Kong series. Nowadays I am constantly learning, trying to improve my skills and love doing so.

My skillset:

C# Language:

I have over 3 years of experience coding with C# in Unity, I have been mostly focussing on Gameplay Development, but have experimented with other means of using it aswell i.e creating small system based algorithms.
